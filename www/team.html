Kelvin W. Nanney , CMP

Kelvin W. Nanney, CMP, is a member manager of Meeting Possibilities, LLC. He has worked in the meetings industry for more than 25 years. He has held management positions in sales and marketing, operations and accounting for Intercontinental, Hilton, Radisson and independent hotel companies in California and Florida.

Kelvin earned his CMP (Certified Meeting Professional) designation January 2001 and was recognized as Meeting Professional of the Quarter by MPI in 2003-2004. Kelvin has an active association management role in Meeting Professionals International, Northern California Chapter and is the administrator for International Special Events Society Northern California Chapter.

Kelvin has been an active member in MPI with memberships in Sacramento, Southern California and North Florida as well as Meeting Professionals International Northern California (MPINCC) serving on the board of directors and as chairman on various committees. Other Memberships include the California Society of Association Executives (CalSAE), the International Association of Association Management Companies (IAAMC), the Walnut Creek and San Ramon Chambers of Commerce, the Tri-Valley Convention and Visitors Bureau and Rotary International.

Diane Schneiderman, MBA, CMP

Diane Schneiderman is co-founder of Meeting Possibilities, LLC. She has over 18 years’ experience in the meetings industry with corporate, association and non-profit clients. During her eight years in the conference industry, she produced over 300 nationwide conferences annually.

Diane’s responsibilities have included curriculum design, staff coordination with top employment law attorneys, writing content for marketing brochures; recruiting high-level moderators and speakers, traveling 25% of the year and managing an annual budget of over $2 million.

Diane has been an active member of the meetings industry professional association, Meeting Professionals International, for many years. As a member of the Northern California Chapter (MPINCC), she has been named a chapter “Power Player” and was nominated for Meeting Professional of the Quarter in fiscal year 2003-2004. Diane has attended three Certified Meeting Professional (CMP) Conclaves, the annual meeting that shapes the testing program for the industry’s certification designation, CMP.

Outside of MPI, Diane is a member of the Walnut Creek Chamber of Commerce Board of Directors. She sits on the John Muir Women’s Health Center Council; is the immediate past Membership Chair of the Executive Women’s Golf Association Northeast Bay Chapter and is part of the team of women business professionals who produced the first annual East Bay Women’s Conference presented by the Walnut Creek Chamber of Commerce.
